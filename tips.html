How to Plant a Tree

  1. Choose a healthy sapling.
  2. Dig a hole twice the size of the root ball.
  3. Place the tree and fill soil.
  4. Water thoroughly.

Tree Maintenance

  • Water regularly.
  • Mulch the base.
  • Prune dead branches.
  • Protect from pests.
